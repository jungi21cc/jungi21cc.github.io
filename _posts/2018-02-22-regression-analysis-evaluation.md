*Ridge*

$
\begin{eqnarray}
\text{cost}
&=& \sum e_i^2 + \lambda \sum w_i^2
\end{eqnarray}
$


*LASSO*

$
\begin{eqnarray}
\text{cost}
&=& \sum e_i^2 + \lambda \sum | w_i |
\end{eqnarray}
$


*Elastic Net*

$
\begin{eqnarray}
\text{cost}
&=& \sum e_i^2 + \lambda_1 \sum | w_i | + \lambda_2 \sum w_i^2
\end{eqnarray}
$
